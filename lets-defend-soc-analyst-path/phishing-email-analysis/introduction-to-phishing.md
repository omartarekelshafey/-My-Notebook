# Introduction to Phishing

**Phishing** is a cyberattack  aimed at stealing personal information by tricking users into clicking malicious links or running harmful files. It occurs during the **"Delivery" phase** of the **Cyber Kill Chain**, where the attacker sends malicious content to the target.

Common tactics include deceptive messages like:

* “You’ve won a gift”
* “Don’t miss the big discount”
* “Your account will be suspended if you don’t click”

Phishing is the **most common initial attack vector**, used to exploit the **human element**, often the weakest link in security. It's typically the first step in gaining access to systems.
